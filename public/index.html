FAQ

Before you upload your first work

Answers to the questions every creator asks before getting started.

What is SeekReap?
+
SeekReap is a blockchain timestamp platform for music, film, and creative works. It generates immutable proof that your work existed at a specific date and time.
Is registration free?
+
Yes. Creating an account is free and includes 1 free certification credit so you can experience the full process at no cost.
What files can I upload?
+
Audio: MP3, WAV, FLAC  ·  Video / Film: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI  ·  Images: PNG, JPG  ·  Documents: PDF
Does SeekReap provide legal proof?
+
The timestamp certificate and blockchain hash provide strong, court-admissible evidence of the existence and authorship of your work at a specific point in time.
Are my files public?
+
No. All works are stored in encrypted private storage. Only you control access and decide whether to share your proof certificate.
