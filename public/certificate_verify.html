How Verification Works
1
Enter the Certificate ID (printed on the certificate) or the blockchain transaction hash. Both uniquely identify a certification record.
2
SeekReap looks up the certificate in our registry and cross-references the SHA-256 hash stored on the Ethereum blockchain.
3
You see the original submission details — work title, certified date, owner, and blockchain proof. Any modification to the original file will produce a different hash and fail verification.
4
The revocation registry confirms whether the certificate is still active or has been disputed/revoked by the issuer or a court order.
